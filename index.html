About Me

EE Student & AI Learner

I am Jia-Kai Dong, a senior student in the Department of Electrical Engineering at National Taiwan University. I have hands-on experience in audio recognition and speech processing, with strong proficiency in Python and fundamental machine learning modeling techniques.

Currently, I am an intern in the LLM Agent Team at Delta Electronics, working on projects related to Model Context Protocol (MCP).

In the summer of 2024, I interned at the Music Information Retrieval Lab at Academia Sinica under Dr. Li Su, where I worked on speech-related machine learning tasks. I am now conducting research at the Speech Processing and Machine Learning Lab under Prof. Hung-Yi Lee, focusing on advanced AI models for speech processing.
